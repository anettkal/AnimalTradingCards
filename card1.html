Red panda

Mozilla's flagship browser, Firefox, means red panda. In 2010 Mozilla adopted two baby red pandas that had been born at Tennessee's Knoxville Zoo.

  • Scientific name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Length: 50 to 64 cm (its tail is 28 to 59 cm)
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Temperate forests

The red panda is slightly larger than a domestic cat with a bear-like body and thick russet fur. Almost 50% of the red panda’s habitat is in the Eastern Himalayas. Primarily an herbivore, the name panda is said to come from the Nepali word ‘ponya,’ which means bamboo or plant eating animal.
